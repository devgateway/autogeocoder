4.5.8 Furthermore, the Yimbaya bus station shall be constructed on an area of 41,000 m2 with the following facilities: (i) loading bays for at least 9 minibuses and 57 taxis for urban transport, 20 buses and 84 minibuses for inter-city transport; (ii) leasable areas composed of 2 refreshment stalls on a surface area of 40 m², 1 toilet on 75 m², 20 telephone booths, a luggage store on 100 m² and 8 shops on 15 m² each.
