4.5 Description of Project Outcomes  4.5.1 The expected project outcomes are summed up as follows:  (i) About 10.7 km of paved road improved between Tombo and Gbessia Airport; 3,407 km of feeder roads developed in Kenien and Bonfi; 8 safe water points installed in the Madina (5) and Bonfi (3) markets;   (ii) 5 (five) interchanges, 2 (two) roundabouts and 7 (seven) pedestrian overpasses constructed; 2 (two) bridges, one for the road and the other for the railway;  (iii) One (1) bus station built in Yimbaya on a surface area of 41 000 m2;
