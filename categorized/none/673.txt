The procurement of consultancy services for technical assistance in the setting up of computer applications for technical calculations, training in the use of such applications as well as retraining in Guinea of NRID and NDDPI staff in engineering techniques, construction of permanent structures, and environmental protection and management for the sum of UA 0.51 million (UA 0.46 million for technical assistance and UA 0.05 million for retraining of NRID technical staff) will be conducted through shopping on the basis of a shortlist of consultancy firms.
