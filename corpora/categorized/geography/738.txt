6.3 Main Risks and Mitigative Measures   6.3.1 Project risks relate to: (1) the bureaucracy within the public contracts management authority; (2) insufficient harmonization in the coordination of project activities between the other donors and the Bank, that could lead to a time overrun in project execution; (3) inadequate RMF resources to ensure the durability of the road network; and (4) slow adoption and implementation of the recommendations of the «Study on traffic improvement in the city of Conakry» conducted in 2002 with AFD financing.
