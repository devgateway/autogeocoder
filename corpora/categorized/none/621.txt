At the technical level, it shall essentially take part in: (i) preparing bidding documents for goods and services and shortlist files for consultancy services; (ii) analysing bids for works, goods and services; (iii) studying works status reports and making recommendations to the NRID; (iv) giving its opinion on all technical proposals of the project made by the control missions, in order to enlighten the Director in decision-taking; and (v) monitoring the execution of works and attending project site meetings.
