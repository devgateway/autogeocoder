On the whole, there are: (i) 2,853 female fishmongers (about 92% of the national total) organized into cooperatives (121), found in the urban districts of Kaloum, Matam and Dixim that are directly connected to the main and feeder roads of the project; and (ii) individual female fishmongers who are generally girls aged 14 to 20 and female family heads (who have an average of 16 to 17 dependent persons).
