According to the Quibb Survey of 2002 on the wellbeing of the population, about 27% of the population in the PA is poor and 13.6% of them are very poor. However, the PA stands out for the high access rate of its household to potable water, which is 93.7% (compared to the national rate of 62%) and 68.8% for covered latrines (compared to national average of 22.7%).
