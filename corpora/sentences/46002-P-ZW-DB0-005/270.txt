5.2  Conditions Associated w Intervention  The grant shall be subject to the following conditions:  (A) Conditions Precedent to Entry into Force of the Protocol of Grant Agreement  The Protocol of Agreement shall enter into force on the date of its signature by the Republic of Zimbabwe and the African Development Fund;  (B) Conditions Precedent to First Disbursement of the Grant  The obligation of the Fund to make the first disbursement of the Grant to the service providers shall be conditional upon the entry into force of the Protocol of Grant Agreement above.
